Payments KYC/AML Rules 2022

§1 Customer Due Diligence

§1.1 Providers must verify identity before activating services; enhanced due diligence applies to high-risk customers.

§2 Retention

§2.1 KYC records shall be retained for at least five (5) years after the business relationship ends.

§3 Data Sharing

§3.1 Sharing with processors is permitted under a written contract imposing confidentiality and security obligations.
