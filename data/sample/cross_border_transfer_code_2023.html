Cross-Border Data Transfer Code 2023

§1 Definitions

§1.1 "Third country" means a country or territory outside the national jurisdiction.

§2 Safeguards

§2.1 Appropriate safeguards include: standard contractual clauses, binding corporate rules, or approved codes of conduct with enforceable commitments.

§2.2 Where relying on explicit consent, the controller must demonstrate that the data subject was informed of the absence of adequacy and of potential risks.

§3 Documentation

§3.1 Controllers shall maintain transfer impact assessments (TIAs) for systematic transfers and review them annually.

§4 Derogations

§4.1 Derogations (e.g., contract necessity) are interpreted narrowly and shall not be used for repetitive, large-scale transfers.
