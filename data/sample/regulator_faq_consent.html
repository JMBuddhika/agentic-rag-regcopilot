Data Protection Authority — FAQ on Consent

§Q1 When is consent valid?

§Q1(a) Consent must be freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous, obtained by a clear affirmative action.

§Q1(b) For high-risk international transfers without adequacy, consent must be explicit and separate from general terms.

§Q2 Can consent be withdrawn?

§Q2(a) Yes. Withdrawal must be as easy as giving consent and shall not affect processing already performed lawfully.

§Q3 Records

§Q3(a) Controllers must keep records of consent, including who, when, how, and what information was provided.
