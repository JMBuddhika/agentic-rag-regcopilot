Guideline on Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA) 2023

§1 When is a DPIA required?

§1.1 A DPIA is required for processing likely to result in high risk, including large-scale monitoring, special-category data at scale, or cross-border systematic transfers.

§2 Minimum Contents

§2.1 A DPIA describes processing operations, assesses necessity and proportionality, identifies risks, and defines measures to address them.

§3 Consultation

§3.1 Where residual high risk remains, the controller shall consult the Authority before processing.
